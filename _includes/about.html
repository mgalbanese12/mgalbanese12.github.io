About

We provide technical services to organizations that make the world a better place. We help smaller firms access a team of experts without the financial commitment of hiring additional personnel.


Whether you need asset valuation and data analysis for contract negotiations, a new enterprise data warehouse and reporting system, or just want to improve your billing practices; we have the knowledge, experience, and network to make it happen!


Michael Albanese has over 10 years of experience working in data science and enjoys partnering with small businesses and community healthcare providers to improve their reporting systems and optimize revenue streams. Michael has broad experience in data analysis and reporting, ranging from financial to healthcare to human resources.


From 2018-2021, Michael worked at the Stanford University School of Medicine as a Financial Analyst before being promoted to the Assistant Division Manager of General Surgery. There he was responsible for managing the financial operations of a division with $25M of clinical, administrative and research funds. Prior to Stanford, Michael worked as the Healthcare Financial and Revenue Analyst for University of Colorado Medicine, where he evaluated and onboarded new clinical practices into the provider network by modeling revenue and creating pro forma financial statements. From 2014-2017 he was a Decision Support Analyst at Colorado Access, a non-profit health plan; where he performed a variety of analyses for multiple lines of business functioning as separate health plans. Before focusing on healthcare operations and reporting, Michael worked at the Federal Reserve Board in Washington, DC as a Financial Analyst; where he loaded, maintained and reported on data for the Federal Reserve System.
